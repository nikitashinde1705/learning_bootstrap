How many steps are there in Lohagad Fort? Lohagad Fort has around 250 to 300 steps, although not all the step sizes are similar.

Lohagad Fort, situated near Lonavala in the Pune district of Maharashtra, is a historic hill fort that attracts history buffs and trekking enthusiasts alike. Perched at an elevation of 1,033 meters above sea level, Lohagad Fort boasts stunning views of the surrounding Sahyadri range and lush green landscapes.

This fort was used to keep the winnings from Surat. Later in Peshwa time Nana Phadnavis used this fort for living for some time and built several structures in the fort such as a big tank and a step-well.
